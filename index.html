About

I joined Advance Ticket GmbH in early 2022 as software developer with a focus on operations and project management. Before that I worked for CSE IT Solutions AG (daycare centers management software) and Techwan SA (public safety and emergency software) as software developer with a parallel education as a fullstack developer (Ruby on Rails). I'm also the co-founder of At3flo, a small third place in the Seeland in Switzerland. After a Bachelor's degree in Micro- and Medicaltechnology at the Bern University of Applied Sciences, I worked for Breitling (watch industry), first as an Industrialization engineer, then for 2 years as a team leader and certified Scrum Master.
